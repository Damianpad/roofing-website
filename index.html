ABOUT US


Protecting your greatest asset, one roof at a time.

Welcome to JJ Builders Roofing and Restoration, a family-owned and operated company with over five years of experience in the roofing industry. We are dedicated to providing high-quality roofing services to our customers in the Dallas Fort-Worth Metroplex.


At JJ Builders Roofing and Restoration, we believe that a roof is more than just a protective layer over your head. It's a vital part of your home's overall structure and the first line of defense against the elements. That's why we take great care in selecting the right materials and using the latest techniques to ensure your roof is strong, durable, and long-lasting.


We look forward to working with you and helping you keep your home safe and protected for years to come. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and get started on your roofing project
